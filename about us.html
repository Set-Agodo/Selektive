Who We Are & What We Do

Our Mission: To replace generic, low-engagement guest lists with intentional communities, ensuring every event is built by the very people who attend it. We started this platform because we were tired of "networking" events where half the guestlist didn't show up, and the people who did felt disconnected. The problem wasn't the event itself; it was the mechanism used to fill the seats. Our unique solution empowers every participant with a vote. We are the only platform that allows nominees to curate the final guest list by choosing who they most want to connect with, transforming simple attendance into a mutual investment.

The Vision & Values

  • Intentional Connection
    • We believe the greatest value lies in the quality of interaction, not just the number of attendees.
  • Power of Choice
    • We believe attendees should have a say in who they connect with.
  • Community-Driven
    • Our platform thrives on collective decision-making to build desired events.
  • Transparency
    • We aim to build trust with both the host and the community.
