The Event Flow: How It Works

Step 1 Create: Design Your Vibe

Set the stage for your event. Define the time, location, theme, and maximum number of guests. Then invite an initial pool of nominees who you think would be great attendees.

  • Set event details and capacity limits
  • Define voting rules and requirements
  • Invite your initial nominee pool
  • Customize event theme and atmosphere
Step 2 Discover: Vote for Your Guests

Browse the event and see all nominees. Attendees vote for who they most want to connect with.

  • Browse event details and fellow nominees
  • Select attendees you want to meet
  • See updated selection list in real-time
  • Top-selected nominees get confirmed spots
Step 3 Connect: Experience the Event

You receive the final guestlist crafted by attendees. Confirmed guests arrive knowing every person was actively chosen by the community.

  • Certified guestlist
  • Connect with highly-desired attendees
  • Enjoy meaningful interactions
  • Build lasting social connections
